Who is Nathan?

After Highscool, I moved from Seattle to the Four Corners region in the late summer of 2016 to do volunteer work in Bible education. I needed a way to work part time to support myself. I noticed that many local small businesses don’t have websites so I decided to learn how to write code, and Web Design quickly became a passion of mine.

I take pride in making sure that the client is happy with my work. My job is to help the client grow their business by offering my skills in professional design and digital media.
